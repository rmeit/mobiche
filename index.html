How to Play Mobius Chess

Welcome to Mobius Chess! The board is twisted into a continuous 3D loop.

  • Topology: Moving off the "top" of the board seamlessly wraps you back around to the "bottom" on the other side.
  • Controls: Click and drag the background to tumble/orbit the 3D board freely. Scroll to zoom.
  • Movement: Click a piece to see its valid moves, then click a highlighted square to move.
  • Pawns: Pawns can move 2 steps forward on their initial move.
  • Rules: Standard chess rules apply! The engine enforces checks, checkmates, stalemates, and pawn promotion.
